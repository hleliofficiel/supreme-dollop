Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2026

database Information Collection

At BebiHost, we collect information to provide better services to all our users—from figuring out basic stuff like which language you speak, to more complex things like which server configurations are most vital to your infrastructure.

Data Categories

  • check_circle Account Information: Name, email address, physical address, and phone number required for provisioning.
  • check_circle Billing Information: Credit card details and billing addresses (processed securely via PCI-DSS compliant providers).
  • check_circle Telemetry Data: Server resource usage, bandwidth metrics, and API access logs.

analytics Use of Data

We use the information we collect from all our services for the following purposes:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our high-performance hosting infrastructure.
  • Develop new features, such as advanced DDoS protection algorithms.
  • Provide personalized services, including customized firewall rule recommendations.
  • Measure performance and analyze how our enterprise tier services are utilized.
  • Communicate with you regarding service disruptions, maintenance windows, or security alerts.

cookie Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies, pixel tags, and similar technologies to ensure our administrative console functions securely and to understand how you interact with our platform.

// Example of session persistence token
Set-Cookie: bebicp_session=xyz123; Secure; HttpOnly; SameSite=Strict

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept essential cookies, you may not be able to use the BebiHost control panel.

handshake Third-Party Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of BebiHost except in the following cases:

With Consent

We'll share personal information outside of BebiHost when we have your explicit consent. For example, registering a domain name via ICANN.

For External Processing

We provide personal information to our trusted affiliates (e.g., Payment Gateways) to process it for us, based on strict instructions and confidentiality agreements.

BebiHost builds security into every layer of our infrastructure to protect your information.

manage_accounts Your Privacy Rights

You can manage your privacy settings directly from the BebiHost Admin Console. Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of your personal data.
  • Request erasure of your personal data (Right to be Forgotten).
  • Object to processing of your personal data.
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data.
  • Request transfer of your personal data.

Need to exercise your rights?

Contact our Data Protection Officer.
